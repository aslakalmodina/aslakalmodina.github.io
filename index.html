Aslak Almadina Establishment For Building Materials is a premier retail, supply, and contracting company based in Dammam, KSA. Specializing in a comprehensive range of building materials and specialized technical services, we serve clients ranging from individual homeowners to large-scale contractors.

WHO WE ARE

About The Company

Established on January 19, 2019, Aslak Almadina has rapidly emerged as a trusted provider of essential building materials and specialized contracting services. We bridge the gap between top-tier manufacturers and the local construction market.

Our expansive operations in Dammam provide easy access to a comprehensive inventory of electricals, plumbing, safety gear, and professional engineering services including finishing and maintenance works.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred supplier of diverse building materials and technical services in the Eastern Province and across KSA, recognized for our quality, reliability, and contribution to smart infrastructure.

Our Mission

To empower builders, contractors, and homeowners by providing immediate access to safe, durable materials and expert services, backed by exceptional customer care and transparent business practices.

Safety is non-negotiable in the building materials industry. At Aslak Almadina, we ensure that every item on our shelves complies with the strict safety standards mandated by the Kingdom.
